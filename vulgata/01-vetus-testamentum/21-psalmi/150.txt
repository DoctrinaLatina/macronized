ALLĒLŪIA: Laudāte Dominum in sānctīs ejus; laudāte eum in firmāmentō virtūtis ejus.
Laudāte eum in virtūtibus ejus; laudāte eum secundum multitūdinem magnitūdinis ejus.
Laudāte eum in sonō tubae; laudāte eum in psaltēriō, et citharā.
Laudāte eum in timpanō, et chorō; laudāte eum in chordīs, et organō.
Laudāte eum in cimbalīs bene sonantibus; laudāte eum in cimbalīs jūbilātiōnis.
Omnis spīritus laudet Dominum. Allēlūia.
