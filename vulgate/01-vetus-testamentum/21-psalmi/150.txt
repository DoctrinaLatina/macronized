ALLĒLŪIA: Laudāte Dominum in sānctīs eius; laudāte eum in firmāmentō virtūtis eius.
Laudāte eum in virtūtibus eius; laudāte eum secundum multitūdinem magnitūdinis eius.
Laudāte eum in sonō tubae; laudāte eum in psaltēriō, et citharā.
Laudāte eum in timpanō, et chorō; laudāte eum in chordīs, et organō.
Laudāte eum in cimbalīs bene sonantibus; laudāte eum in cimbalīs iūbilātiōnis.
Omnis spīritus laudet Dominum. Allēlūia.
